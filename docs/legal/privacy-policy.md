# Privacy Policy

Intel Trader Technologies Limited (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy and complies with the Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (“NDPR”), Section 30 of the Nigeria Data Protection Bill, and industry best practices. This Privacy Policy describes the categories of personal data we process, how we process it, your rights, and the safeguards we maintain.

### 1. Personal Data Collected
We collect the following categories of data:

- **Identity and Contact Data:** full name, username, email address, phone number, country, organization name, and any information provided during registration or profile updates.  
- **Credential Data:** password hashes, salts, password reset tokens, and any biometric or multi-factor data when you enable MFA.  
- **Authentication Metadata:** login timestamps, IP addresses, device/browser fingerprints, and session cookies logged by NextAuth.  
- **Usage and Analytics Data:** dashboard interactions, feature access timestamps, usage logs, websocket events, API request logs, and telemetry for monitoring service health.  
- **Payment and Billing Data:** payment instrument metadata stored by Stripe (card bin, expiry month/year, country), recurring billing status, subscription tier, invoices, and any tax identifiers required under Nigerian VAT/FIRS rules.  
- **Support and Communication Records:** messages submitted via support, feedback forms, chat transcripts, and email correspondence.

### 2. Lawful Bases and Purposes
We process your data under the following lawful bases:

- **Performance of a Contract** – to create your account, deliver subscriptions, process billing, and provide the services you request.  
- **Legitimate Interests** – to operate, secure, monitor, and improve the Platform (e.g., usage logs, anomaly detection, AI training) while balancing your fundamental rights.  
- **Compliance with Legal Obligations** – to adhere to Nigerian anti-money laundering, tax, or regulatory reporting requirements.  
- **Consent** – for marketing communications, cookies, or when explicitly requested; you may withdraw consent at any time.

### 3. Data Sharing and Transfers
We share data only with: (a) service providers acting on our behalf—e.g., NextAuth for authentication, Stripe for payments, Prisma for database access, Socket.IO for real-time feeds, Vercel/Cloudflare for hosting, Segment/Amplitude for analytics—under binding data processing agreements; (b) professional advisers when required by law; and (c) third parties in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice). We may transfer data to partners outside Nigeria (e.g., AWS/EU) but only when safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions, are in place. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you may request the specific safeguards used for these transfers.

### 4. Data Security and Retention
We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures (encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, regular audits, SOC 2-style controls) to protect your data. Usage logs are retained for sixty (60) days, or longer if required for analytics or compliance. Account information, including billing records, may be retained for seven (7) years to satisfy tax and statutory obligations. We securely erase backups and logs once retention periods expire.

### 5. Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies and similar technologies as detailed in our Cookie Policy. Session cookies are essential to keep you signed in; analytics cookies help us improve performance. You may block cookies via browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

### 6. Your NDPR Rights
Under the NDPR, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you;  
- Request correction, deletion, or portability;  
- Object to or restrict processing;  
- Withdraw consent (where processing is consent‑based);  
- Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) if you believe your rights were breached.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@cacsms.com or use the dashboard. We respond within 30 days unless expedited by law.

### 7. Children and Minors
Our services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us at privacy@cacsms.com; we will delete it promptly.

### 8. Changes to Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in law, practice, or Platform features. We will post the updated version on the Platform with the “last updated” date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
