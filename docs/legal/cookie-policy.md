# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Intel Trader uses cookies, web beacons, and similar local storage technologies when you visit landing pages, dashboards, or embedded support tools at inteltrader.cacsms.com. Cookies are small text files stored on your browser, device, or local storage that help us deliver personalized experiences while respecting NDPR requirements.

## Types of Cookies We Use

1. **Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary):** Facilitate basic platform functions such as authentication, session persistence (NextAuth’s JWT cookies), load balancing, and consent recall. They cannot be disabled without disrupting usage and are retained only for the duration of your session or until logout.

2. **Performance Cookies:** Collect anonymous statistics about page views, feature usage, and load times for Lagos and Abuja product teams. Data is aggregated via encrypted telemetry pipelines and old entries are purged after 24 months.

3. **Functional Cookies:** Store settings such as preferred language, layout arrangements, and alert frequency so Nigerian teams don’t lose their workspace configuration between logins.

4. **Targeting/Marketing Cookies:** Used only when you opt in—for example, retargeting past visitors with compliance webinars. You can withdraw consent at any time via browser settings or by emailing legal@cacsms.com.

## Third-Party Cookies

We integrate carefully selected third parties (e.g., analytics, monitoring) that also commit to NDPR compliance. Each partner signs data processing agreements, maintains encryption, and restricts data to aggregated, pseudonymized forms.

## Managing Cookies

You control cookies through:

- Browser preferences (Safari, Chrome, Edge) where you can block, delete, or inspect cookies.
- The platform’s cookie banner, which records your choices in a persistent cookie.
- Emailing legal@cacsms.com to request assistance with granular consent or compliance questions.

## Updates to the Cookie Policy

Changes are posted with a revision date. If major changes occur, we notify logged-in users and publish a summary via the legal@cacsms.com contact channel.
